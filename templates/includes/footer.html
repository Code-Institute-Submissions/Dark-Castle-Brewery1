Important Shipping & Returns info

At Dark Castle brewery, we aim to dispatch your beer within 3 to 5 working days. Please contact us if you need an order urgently and we will endeavour to provide a faster turnaround.

We use Fastway couriers for local and national deliveries. If for some reason you are not happy with the product you receive, we will provide a refund within 30 days.

Refunds can only be provided on beer that has not been opened. To process your refund, email info@darkcastlebrewery.ie for instructions. The beer must be returned unopened to our brewery in Cork City. The return shipping cost of items is the responsibility of the purchaser. Once we have received the items in the condition that they were sent, we will refund within 30 days, less postage charges. All refunds will be issued through original method of payment.

Please note our shipping and returns policy is subject to change
